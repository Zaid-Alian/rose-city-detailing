Rose City Detailing — Brand Color Palette

Primary (deep burgundy): #2d1f2b  — Headings, text
Nav/Dark background:     #1a1218  — Navbar, footer, comparison labels

Border:                  #e8d8de  — Soft pink-grey for card borders

Muted text:              #7a6472  — Warm mauve-grey for secondary text
